about

Alexa Rae Rosenberg is a current student at Sunny Purchase
Opera Conservatory

She has performed the role of Hänsel und
Gretel in Humperdinck’s Hänsel und Gretel at Purchase Opera. Alexa has also appeared in Britten’s A Midsummer
Night’s Dream, Le Nozze di Figaro and The Crucible by Robert Ward. She was also
a soloist in Handel’s Messiah and Vivaldi’s Gloria with Purchase Symphony
Orchestra and Purchase Chorus.

She worked with Jay Lesenger in the Manhattan School of Music’s production
of Street Scene by Kurt Weill. Alexa has also studied acting at Stella Adler. She is
more than excited to be singing the 6th Spirit with Utopia Opera.
